In order to make possible for you to use certain functions of Slipe, specifically finding the best focus point automatically, we will process (provided by you) human face images (photo or video) through Vision Face Detection API technologies that will provide us information about human faces’ position on your image and/or video frame. For more information about Vision Face Detection API technology we use, you may visit: Face Detection Documentation.

  • use the Face Data for authentication, advertising, or marketing purposes, or to otherwise target a User in a similar manner;
  • use the Face Data to build a User profile, or otherwise attempt, facilitate, or encourage third parties to identify anonymous Users or reconstruct User profiles based on the Face Data;

You hereby express your informed written consent on processing of your Face Data.
